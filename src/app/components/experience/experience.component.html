My Journey in Tech

“I have always been passionate about technology. My journey began with Android development, where I spent six years building and optimizing mobile applications. One of my most exciting projects was the Rizzle short video mobile app and its web version. Seeing people create and engage with short videos on a platform I helped build was truly rewarding.”

“After that, I worked on Android Ama App, exploring new ways to enhance user experience. Then came Fishwrap and Fashionwrap, where I blended content, fashion, and technology. Each project pushed me to think differently and solve unique challenges.”

“As I progressed, I moved into backend development and spent six years mastering Node.js. I worked with Redis, AWS, Kafka, GraphQL, ElasticSearch, and many other technologies. I integrated Stripe for payments, set up AWS services for email and SMS, and even used OpenAI for summarization. Every project taught me something new.”

“Beyond coding, I stepped into leadership roles. I started mentoring developers, setting up CI/CD pipelines, and ensuring high-quality code. Managing a team is not just about writing clean code—it’s about guiding people, solving problems, and driving efficiency.”

“Recently, I joined OpenText as a Lead Software Engineer for the LoadRunner team. Now, I work at the intersection of AI and backend engineering, making architectural decisions and leading teams.”

“The journey hasn’t always been easy. I’ve faced tight deadlines, production issues, and even self-doubt. I struggled with stage fear in meetings and felt pressure in new leadership roles. But I pushed myself, learning step by step. Today, I’m more confident in public speaking and decision-making than ever before.”

“On the financial side, I’ve planned well. I have a strategy involving mutual funds, fixed deposits, and real estate, ensuring a secure future for my family. Balancing work, life, and financial goals has been a key part of my success.”
